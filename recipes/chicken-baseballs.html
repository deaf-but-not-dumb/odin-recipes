Chicken Baseballs

Description

Great for kids. You can add half to a full packet of dry ranch dressing to the filling for yet more flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tsp margarine
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
  • 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
  • 2 tbsp bread crumbs

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Stir together the cream cheese, margarine, salt pepper, milk, and onions
  3. Fold the chicken into the cream cheese mixture
  4. Separate crescent rolls into 4 rectangles and smooth the perforations
  5. Spoon 1/2 cup of the chicken mixture into the center of each rectangle
  6. Fold the corners over the chicken mixture and pinch along the edges to seal into a ball shape. Arrange on a baking sheet.
  7. Sprinkle bread crumbs over the top of the newly formed "baseballs"
  8. Bake in preheated oven until browned, about 20 minutes
